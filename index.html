Our Services

Silk Road Real Estate offers our clients a professional maintenance services for their property. The aim is to have your assets ready, on short notice, should you require it. This allows you to maximize their potential. Regular maintenance saves you money by avoiding big and costly disasters

Research Services

The Silk Road Research Department is our intellectual core; we provide business plans, feasibility studies, industry and sector reports, forensic evaluation, management consultancy and other bespoke research services.

Legal Consultancy

We are not a law firm, but we do have a panel of reputable Sri Lankan lawyers to recommend, depending on the specific issue. Separately, we offer our expertise in Human Resources Management, gaining permits and documentation.

Company Secretarial

Our experienced and highly qualified Company Secretarial team will establish your company and act on your behalf to ensure ongoing regulatory compliance.

Financial Services

Using the latest online financial products, we offer payroll and social security services, monthly management accounting and preparation of annual financial statements. We work closely with independent Auditors and expert Tax Advisors to ensure you are getting the best guidance.
